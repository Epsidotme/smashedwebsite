About Smashed

Smashed is a VRChat nightlife community built for neon nights, social chaos, and endless games. From the Event Hall with its DJ stage and massive card table to the Mixed Games Room (Beerpong, Spin the Wheel, Pool, and a full Arcade), there’s always something to do.

Challenge your friends in 1v1 — Chess, Checkers, Connect 4, Buckshot Roulette — or relax upstairs in VIP and VIP+ lounges. Join the group, catch events, and help fuel the party on Patreon.

💎 VRCLINK FOR VIP+

Run stronger groups with VRCLINK — included with VIP+

VIP+ adds a complete group toolkit powered by VRCLINK, so you can manage your presence in‑world and on the web without extra plugins or hacks. It all works with your existing Smashed setup.

What you get

  • Poster control — swap your group poster any time for instant updates.
  • Gallery takeovers — upload up to six images; they’re atlased and rotate on the community board.
  • Moderator power — up to ten moderators, with regular VIPs not counting toward the limit.
  • Reach insights — unique visitor stats to understand real impact.
  • Extra in‑world links — go beyond the default VRChat group page.
  • Custom group page — centralize info, rules, mods and all your links.
  • Tournaments — bracket system built in; live tournament world display is on the roadmap.

Getting started

  1. Go to vrclink.com.
  2. Connect with Patreon to unlock your VIP+ perks.
  3. Set up your group visuals, links and moderators.
  4. In‑world: open your portable menu → Group Host to enable the group you moderate (one active at a time).

We’re continuing to expand VIP+ features — feedback welcome.
